# Cross-Volume Victim Testimony Mapping

*This document maps redacted victim designations in the VOL00012 SDNY prosecution memo to designations used in other EFTA volumes and in [rhowardstone/Epstein-research](https://github.com/rhowardstone/Epstein-research). No real names are used. All context details are from OCR text extraction verified against original PDFs.*

**Ethical note:** The goal is to connect testimony across volumes — showing which depositions, FBI 302s, and grand jury transcripts relate to the same individuals who appear in our prosecution memo — not to publicly identify any victim. All names remain redacted.

## Victim Designations in VOL00012 Prosecution Memo

The SDNY prosecution memo (EFTA02731082.pdf, 86 pages) uses four numbered victim designations. An additional unnumbered victim provides the "lent out" testimony. These designations are specific to this memo and differ from the Maxwell trial designations (Minor Victim-1 through 5).

### Victim-1 (New York recruiter)

**Contextual fingerprint** (from OCR, ren16.txt lines 3866–3964):
- Recruited at approximately age 14
- Living alone in New York City, supporting herself
- Met another teenager at a New York City club who described "topless massages" for a rich man
- Visited Epstein's Manhattan mansion dozens of times (~2002–2005)
- Paid $300 per massage, $300 per girl recruited
- Told Epstein her true age (14)
- Recruited "dozens of girls" over approximately two years
- Scheduled appointments through "Leslie" (identified as Leslie Groff)
- Described Epstein's residence in detail: artwork in massage room, stuffed dog, library compared to "Beauty and the Beast"
- EFTA pages: 02731041–02731043 (Source: EFTA02731082.pdf pages 4–6)

**Additional details** (ren16.txt lines 3900–3911):
- Epstein told her he did not like dark-skinned girls
- As Victim-1 grew older and knew fewer young girls, she brought older girls, which Epstein expressed displeasure about
- Recalled at least one girl showing Epstein an ID to prove she was under 18

**Corroboration noted by SDNY:**
- Phone message from 646 area code with Victim-1's first name found in trash pull from FL residence
- Individual-1 and Individual-2 (introduced to Epstein by Victim-1) confirmed the pattern in prior FL interviews
- Account is "nearly identical" to Victim-2's account

### Victim-2 (Florida recruiter)

**Contextual fingerprint** (from OCR, ren16.txt lines 3992–4067):
- Recruited at approximately age 14 (recalled 14; stated 15 in 2007 FBI interview)
- High school dropout, living away from home, supporting herself
- Recruited by a young girl at a party: could make $200 for a massage
- Went to Epstein's Palm Beach residence
- Performed 10+ sexualized massages
- Recruited approximately 20–30 additional girls, all approximately 15–20 years old
- Initially told to say she was 18; later stopped because Epstein clearly preferred younger girls
- Wore braces; "it would have been obvious that she was very young"
- Coordinated appointments with [redacted, likely Groff based on phone records]
- When FL investigations became public, received a phone call stating she would be "taken care of if she didn't say anything"
- Full name and phone number appear on message pads recovered from search of Epstein's FL residence
- EFTA pages: 02731043–02731045 (Source: EFTA02731082.pdf pages 6–8)

### Victim-3 (Florida, bank records)

**Contextual fingerprint** (from OCR, ren16.txt lines 4076–4131):
- Between ~2003 and 2005, visited Epstein's FL residence approximately 20 times
- Paid $200 per visit
- Told Epstein she was 17, but SDNY believes she may have been 16 based on bank deposit dates
- Deposits began approximately June 2003 (weeks past her 16th birthday)
- Cash payments continued through January 2005

- Epstein promised travel and a modeling career after she turned 18

- Brought two 17-year-old girls to Epstein's house
- Phone records corroborate: calls between [redacted] and Victim-3 match Epstein's flight patterns (e.g., September 16, 2004: calls in afternoon, then flight JFK→West Palm Beach that night)
- EFTA pages: 02731045–02731046 (Source: EFTA02731082.pdf pages 8–9)

### Victim-4 (Florida, single incident)

**Contextual fingerprint** (from OCR, ren16.txt lines 4147–4157):

### The "Lent Out" Victim (unnumbered, Florida/NY/USVI)

**Contextual fingerprint** (from OCR, ren16.txt lines 9003–9295):

  - **Harvey Weinstein**: Epstein instructed to massage at Paris residence; Weinstein ordered her to remove shirt (EFTA02731096 — different section/different redacted victim name for this passage)

  - **Jes Staley**: Epstein directed massage; Staley "raped" her (EFTA02731114)

### Potential Mappings: Our Victim → Maxwell Trial Designation

**Victim-3 (our memo) → Minor Victim-3 (Maxwell trial)** — HIGH CONFIDENCE

Context match (4 points):
1. Both are Florida-based victims
2. Both have cell phone records corroborating massage scheduling with co-conspirator
3. Minor Victim-3's key reference is EFTA02731039 page 8, which is the same VOL00012 prosecution memo series as our Victim-3
4. Our Victim-3's phone record detail (September 16, 2004 calls matching flight JFK→WPB) is consistent with the "calls during Epstein NYC trips" description

This is the strongest mapping because the phone record corroboration is a distinctive factual detail unique to one victim.

**Victim-1 (our memo) → No clear Maxwell trial match** — NONE

Victim-1 is a New York recruiter, age 14, 2002–2005. The Maxwell trial Minor Victims are focused on the 1994–2004 period with emphasis on Florida and travel. Victim-1 may correspond to a witness rather than a charged victim in the Maxwell case.

**Victim-2 (our memo) → Possible Minor Victim-1 or Minor Victim-2** — LOW CONFIDENCE

Both Minor Victim-1 and Minor Victim-2 are Florida-based. Without more context on their age or recruitment method, the match cannot be scored higher.

**"Lent Out" Victim → No Maxwell trial designation** — SPECULATIVE

The "lent out" victim appears primarily in the proffer sections (pages 55–59) of the prosecution memo that deals with post-arrest interviews (August–October 2019). Her testimony about being lent out to Dubin, Andrew, Black, Staley etc. occurred during these proffer sessions. The Maxwell trial designations (Minor Victim-1 through 5) were for charging purposes, and this victim's "lent out" testimony was not charged. SDNY's inability to corroborate the lent-out accounts may explain why this victim was not designated as a Minor Victim in the Maxwell trial.

## Limitations

1. **Different numbering systems.** "Victim-1" in the prosecution memo is NOT the same as "Minor Victim-1" in the Maxwell trial. The prosecution memo was written pre-trial; the Maxwell trial designations were created for charging purposes.

2. **Redaction prevents definitive matching.** All names are redacted in the prosecution memo. Context matching is the only available method, and it can produce false positives.

3. **The pseudonym registry is not victim-focused.** Rhowardstone's 273-entry Pseudonym Codename Registry maps associates, lawyers, email aliases, and codenames — not victim designations. Victim numbering systems are tracked in their VICTIM_CENSUS.md instead.

4. **The person registry has only 1 victim entry.** The 1,614-person registry categorizes almost everyone as "associate" rather than "victim," likely for ethical/privacy reasons. Victim data is in the census and investigation reports, not the structured registry.

5. **SDNY's uncorroborated testimony.** The "lent out" accounts are the most significant allegations in the memo, but SDNY explicitly states they could not corroborate them. Cross-volume documents from DS9 show the investigation continued post-memo, but the corroboration status remains uncertain.

6. **One-direction mapping only.** We matched our victims to rhowardstone's data. We did not search for rhowardstone victims that might NOT appear in our memo — there may be victims documented in other volumes who are absent from the VOL00012 prosecution memos.

## Methodology

1. Extracted all victim references from ren16.txt and ren17.txt using grep for "Victim-1" through "Victim-4", "Minor Victim", and "lent out". Recorded contextual fingerprints: age, location, recruitment method, associates named, unique details, approximate dates, EFTA pages (corrected for off-by-one).

2. Loaded rhowardstone's VICTIM_CENSUS.md, which maps Maxwell trial designations (Minor Victim-1 through 5), Jane Doe designations (10+), and prosecution memo victim-witness designations across all EFTA volumes.

3. Loaded rhowardstone's persons_registry.json (1,614 entries). Found only 1 victim-category entry — victim data is stored in investigation reports, not the registry.

4. Checked rhowardstone's Pseudonym Codename Registry (1,009 lines). No victim-to-pseudonym mappings; registry covers associates and codenames.

5. Cross-referenced victim contextual fingerprints against rhowardstone's data: matched on age, location, phone records, recruitment method, named associates, and unique details. Scored matches as HIGH (3+ context points), MEDIUM (2), LOW (1), or NONE.

6. For HIGH matches, identified cross-volume EFTA documents using rhowardstone's evidence tables.
