# The Proffer Landscape: SDNY Interviewed Everyone and Charged One Person

*Every claim is sourced to specific EFTA documents. The Maxwell 2025 proffer is publicly available at [justice.gov/maxwell-interview](https://www.justice.gov/maxwell-interview). The Groff proffer (EFTA01682023) and the complete 78-proffer mapping are, to our knowledge, not documented elsewhere.*

## Summary

A systematic search of the rhowardstone full-text corpus (2.89 million pages across all EFTA datasets) identified **78 proffer agreements** spanning April 2019 through July 2025. Of these, 7 name the profferor; 71 are redacted. Named profferors include Leslie Groff, Prince Andrew, Lawrence Visoski (chief pilot), David Rodgers (co-pilot), Richard Barnett, and Ghislaine Maxwell.

Only Ghislaine Maxwell was indicted. Sarah Kellen had plea negotiations that collapsed. Everyone else who proffered was never charged.

## The Complete Chronological Timeline

### Pre-Arrest: Building the Case (April–May 2019)

Six proffers before Epstein's arrest — all dates redacted, all female. These are likely victims providing testimony to build the indictment. (Sources: EFTA01247320, EFTA01682025, EFTA01247302, EFTA01247310, EFTA00090773, EFTA01247322)

### The Arrest and Its Aftermath (July 2019)

- **July 2, 2019:** Grand jury returns indictment
- **July 6, 2019:** Epstein arrested at Teterboro
- **July 12, 2019:** Three proffers on a single day, 10 days post-arrest. One attorney (Joseph Nascimento) represents at least two female clients. (Sources: EFTA00024704, EFTA00027643, EFTA00159466)
- **July 17, 2019:** Another female proffer (EFTA01245647)
- **July 18, 2019:** SDNY gives Groff a reverse proffer. Her attorney refuses. Fifth Amendment invoked. (Source: EFTA02731146)

### The Wave: August–September 2019

The most intensive period: **approximately 25 proffers in 7 weeks**. SDNY was conducting 3-4 proffers per week. Multiple sessions occurred on the same day (August 16, 19, 21, 26, 28; September 5, 9, 10). Some may be duplicates across datasets, but the scale is clear: SDNY mounted a massive post-arrest interview campaign.

- **August 10, 2019:** Epstein dies in custody — the proffers continue without interruption
- **September 16, 2019:** Richard Barnett proffers (Source: EFTA01245368)
- **October 2019:** Prosecution memo written, reporting Groff's proffer as "pending"

### The Named Proffers (2020–2021)

- **February 7, 2020:** David Rodgers (co-pilot) proffers with attorney Brendan Quigley (Source: EFTA00159178)
- **October 7, 2020:** Lawrence Visoski (chief pilot) proffers by video conference. Rhowardstone's analysis shows this was specifically about CBP officer Timothy Routch and customs clearance patterns, not the trafficking itself. Visoski was planned as a Maxwell trial witness. (Source: EFTA00159723; rhowardstone CBP_CORRUPTION_INVESTIGATION.md, credited)
- **July 23, 2021:** **Leslie Groff proffers** with attorney Michael Bachner — 21 months after initially refusing. Standard queen-for-a-day terms. "THIS IS NOT A COOPERATION AGREEMENT." Continuation dates suggest multiple sessions. This proffer is not cited in rhowardstone's 165-report project. (Source: EFTA01682023)

### Maxwell Trial and Beyond

- **November 2021–December 2021:** Maxwell trial. She is convicted.
- **June 2022:** Maxwell sentenced to 20 years.
- **July 24–25, 2025:** Maxwell proffers with Deputy Attorney General Todd Blanche at the Northern District of Florida (Tallahassee). The full transcript and audio were publicly released by the DOJ in August 2025 and are available at [justice.gov/maxwell-interview](https://www.justice.gov/maxwell-interview). This is widely reported and is not a finding of this project. (Source: DOJ-OGR-00022982)

### Prince Andrew

A proffer agreement document exists at EFTA00101307, naming "H.R.H. Prince Andrew Albert Christian Edward, the Duke of York, also known as Andrew Mountbatten-Windsor" as the client. His attorney is identified as "Gary" — rhowardstone identifies Andrew's UK counsel as Gary Bloxsome of Blackfords LLP. The date is redacted. Rhowardstone extensively documents months of MLAT negotiations and Bloxsome's obstruction of SDNY interview requests, but does not cite this specific document. Whether the proffer was executed or only drafted cannot be confirmed from the document alone. (Sources: EFTA00101307; rhowardstone PROSECUTION_FAILURES_ANALYSIS.md, LAWYERS_LITIGATION_INDEX.md, credited)

## The Groff Arc

The Groff proffer resolves an open question from the prosecution memo but creates a larger one.

**October 2019** (prosecution memo): "He appeared to understand our position and is considering whether to bring Groff in for a proffer." (Source: EFTA02731146)

**July 23, 2021** (proffer agreement): "With respect to the meeting of Lesley Groff 'Client' and her attorney, Michael Bachner, Esq., with Assistant United States Attorney [redacted] to be held on July 23, 2021" (Source: EFTA01682023)

Twenty-one months between the memo and the proffer. In between: Epstein died, Maxwell was arrested, the investigation continued. Groff eventually sat down. What she said is protected under the proffer terms. What SDNY did with her information is unknown. She was never charged.

The proffer agreement explicitly states: "The Government does not agree to make a motion on the Client's behalf or to enter into a cooperation agreement, plea agreement, immunity or non-prosecution agreement." This means the proffer was not, by its terms, a deal. But it also means SDNY received Groff's account of events — and still chose not to prosecute.

## What This Mapping Shows

The proffer landscape reveals an investigation that was far more active than the prosecution memo or public record suggests. SDNY conducted dozens of formal proffer sessions across two years. They got cooperation from both pilots, from Groff (who initially refused), and from numerous redacted individuals who were likely victims and witnesses.

The question is not whether SDNY investigated. They did — intensively, systematically, and with formal legal agreements. The question is why the investigation produced proffers but not indictments for anyone except Maxwell.

## Limitations

1. **Many proffers may be duplicates.** The same proffer agreement filed in multiple datasets (DS8, DS9) produces multiple hits. The 78 total likely represents 40-50 unique sessions.

2. **Redacted identifications are speculative.** The 71 redacted proffers could not be reliably identified through attorney bridging, temporal matching, or document clustering. Most had all identifying information redacted.

3. **Proffer ≠ cooperation.** Every proffer agreement explicitly states "THIS IS NOT A COOPERATION AGREEMENT." A proffer provides information to the government under limited use restrictions. It does not mean the profferor cooperated meaningfully or that their information was useful.

4. **The prosecution memo predates most named proffers.** The memo was written ~October 2019. Groff proffered July 2021. Visoski proffered October 2020. The memo's characterization of these people may not reflect what SDNY learned after these proffers.

5. **The Maxwell 2025 proffer is public record.** It was released by the DOJ, reported by NPR, The Hill, Slate, and others, and discussed by the Senate Judiciary Committee. It is included here for completeness, not as a discovery.

6. **We cannot know what anyone said in their proffer.** Proffer content is protected. We know the meetings happened. We do not know what was discussed, whether the information was useful, or whether it led to any action.
